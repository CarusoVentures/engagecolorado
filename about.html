About Engage Colorado

What this is

Engage Colorado is an independent publishing platform focused on articles, newsletters, and commentary about Colorado's future — its leadership, public policy, economic opportunity, and long-term success.

This platform was established as a vehicle to support thoughtful, substantive public engagement with the ideas and initiatives shaping our state. It is designed to provide flexibility of authorship and perspective, allowing contributors to write freely and without institutional constraint.

Independence

Engage Colorado is not the Engage Coalition, and is not affiliated with any coalition, political organization, or advocacy group. It is an independent publishing vehicle. The views expressed in articles and newsletters are those of their individual authors.

This independence is intentional. It gives contributors the room to engage honestly with complex policy questions — without the constraints that come with coalition membership or organizational affiliation.

What to expect

This platform is in its early stages. Additional content — including regular newsletters, long-form commentary, and resources — will be added over time. Subscribe to stay informed as new material is published.
