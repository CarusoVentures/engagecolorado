Introducing Engage Colorado: An Independent Platform for Colorado's Future

Why we built this platform, what it's for, and how it differs from the coalitions and organizations working alongside it.

The Editors · April 2025

Colorado is at an inflection point. The decisions being made right now — about health care access, economic opportunity, housing, and the shape of state government — will define life here for a generation. The conversations happening in policy circles, in the legislature, and in communities across the state deserve serious, sustained public engagement.

That is why we built Engage Colorado.

This platform was established as an independent vehicle for publishing articles, newsletters, and commentary focused on Colorado's future. It is not a coalition, an advocacy group, or a campaign. It is a publishing platform — designed to give writers, thinkers, and practitioners the space to engage publicly with the ideas and initiatives shaping our state.

Why independence matters

Coalition work is valuable. But coalitions come with constraints: shared messaging, institutional positioning, and the inevitable compromises that come from representing many stakeholders at once. Good public writing sometimes requires more freedom than that.

"Honest engagement with policy questions requires the ability to say things that coalitions cannot always say — and to say them plainly."

Engage Colorado is intentionally separate from the Engage Coalition. This is not a criticism of coalition work — it is a recognition that different vehicles serve different purposes. This platform exists to provide flexibility of authorship, perspective, and content that a coalition structure cannot always accommodate.

What you'll find here

In the coming months, we'll be publishing newsletters, analysis, and commentary on the policy questions that matter most to Colorado. We'll link to and contextualize the work happening at initiatives like Ensuring Colorado and others. And we'll give contributors a home for writing that doesn't fit neatly anywhere else.

We're at an early stage. More content is coming. Subscribe below to stay informed as new material is published.
